"   The way that may be traversed is not the Eternal WAY. The name which can be uttered is not the Eternal NAME.  Without name \u2014 Heaven and Earth (Nature) at the beginning were called the mother of all things. Thus it always is that (he who is) without passion can grasp the inner essence, while (he who is blinded) by passion can only apprehend the outer form. These two have really the same issue, and differ only in name. Together they are spoken of as the First Cause. The cause of the First Cause itself is the gateway of the Essential."
