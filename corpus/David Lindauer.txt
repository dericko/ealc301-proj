"   A tao that one can tao     Is not the entire tao A name that one can name     Is not the entire name.  In the absence of names     Lies the origin of heavens and earth The presence of names     Is mother to the 10000 things.  So viewing entireness without desire     One may see subtleties Viewing entireness with desire     One may see boundaries.  That which is these both     Goes about as itself yet there are different names. Categorizing them together there is insight     Very deep insight The gateway to collective subtleties."
