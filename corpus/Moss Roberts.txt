"    The Way as \u201cway\u201d bespeaks no common lasting Way, The name as \u201cname\u201d no common lasting name. Absent is the name for sky and land\u2019s first life, Present for the mother of all ten thousand things. Desire ever-absent: Behold the seed germs of all things. Desire ever-present: Behold their every finite course. Forth together came the two As one and the same But differ in name. As one, a dark recess That probed recedes Past that portal whence The milling seed germs teem."
